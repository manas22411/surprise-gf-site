My last message

For you, always

I’m not moving on. I’m not letting you go. I’m only stopping because I can’t bear to hurt you anymore—not because my heart has let you go. If you ever reach out, I’ll come running to you, faster than ever. I check my phone countless times, hoping it’s you. I don’t say “gudiaa,” “gudoo,” “choti Aarushi chaiye,” “chote pau,” or “meri choti se bhau” anymore, but you—and those words—are still my last thoughts before I fall asleep every night. I miss you, every single night. I want you deeply, but sometimes it hurts more because everything I do keeps me stuck in the same place. Just know this: even if I move on, even if life takes me elsewhere, you’ll always have a space in my heart. Sometimes, I share an Instagram post on WhatsApp that feels like it’s written for me, for us—but it’s already too late now, gudiaa, meri jaan, meri bhau. I love you in ways I can’t even describe, and maybe that doesn’t change anything, but I’ve realized it’s already too late.
